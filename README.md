The projects consists of three users mainly: patient,doctor and medical aid provider.
the patient will be able to create account,enter the medical aid and make appointment with doctor and also do followup on the system reqarding medication to be collected if it's finished.
the medical aid provider will validate the patient as one of their patient and also will also receive the bill from the doctor.
The doctor will be able to view the list of the active patients,a patients becomes active after the medical aid provider has validated them,the doctor will also confirm the availability to the appointments.
the doctor will also be able to conclude the appointment success,and can also delete certain patients from their patient list.
